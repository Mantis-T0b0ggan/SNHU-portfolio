04 – M.C Perkin's Cove Reservation System

The artifact that I chose in 04 and 05 is the reservation system that I created in IT-415 and IT-420. The reservation system was created for a restaurant needed a complete overhaul of the old system which was simply pen and paper. The system was designed in IT-415 and was quite comprehensive. The system needed to be able to make it easy for the person taking the reservation to quickly put the information into the system.

This artifact demonstrates effective system design and maintenance for the entire system. The document is quite long and comprehensive. However, the document shows the entire design process from the front-end GUI, the fill-in form and the back-end database and the data tables. A cloud-based database was selected to reduce overhead cost.

The skills that I was able to learn from the project are invaluable. I was able to utilize my knowledge from multiple courses such as creating a UML design of the system and the data tables. I was able to use knowledge from a business design course to ensure the system and the designs were practical and business ready. Also, that the entire system document was created in such a way that it could be used to present the system to upper management of a company.
